# Summer Springboard Program Overview - UC Berkeley 2026

## About Summer Springboard

Summer Springboard (SSB) is a pre-college academic program offered at UC Berkeley and other prestigious universities. The program provides middle school and high school students with immersive, college-level academic experiences in various fields.

### Tagline
**"Look Inward. Go Upward."**

### Program Organization
- **Parent Organization:** Terra Education Travel
- **Program Name:** Summer Springboard
- **2026 Location:** University of California, Berkeley

## Daily Schedule Structure

### Typical Academic Day (Monday-Friday)

**Morning:**
- Commuter students check-in: 8:30am
- Breakfast: Crossroads Dining Hall (with mentor groups)
- Morning academic sessions

**Afternoon:**
- Afternoon academic sessions
- College Readiness Workshops
- Project work time

**Evening:**
- Dinner: 6:00pm-7:00pm (with mentor groups)
- Night activities: 7:00pm-9:30pm
- Unstructured time: 8:30pm-9:30pm (residential students only)
- Back to rooms: 9:30pm
- Bed checks: 10:30pm
- Commuter departure: After evening program ends

- Team huddle: 10:30pm-11:00pm (after bed checks)
