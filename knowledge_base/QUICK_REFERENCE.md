## Earthquake Quick Response
1. **DROP** to floor
2. **COVER** under sturdy furniture
3. **HOLD ON** until shaking stops
4. **EVACUATE** building immediately after
5. **GO TO:** Emergency Assembly Area (EAA)

## Active Shooter Quick Response
**RUN-HIDE-FIGHT**
1. **RUN:** If safe, evacuate immediately. Call 911 when safe.
2. **HIDE:** If can't run - lock doors, barricade, lights off, silence phones, seek cover
3. **FIGHT:** Last resort - grab any object to incapacitate attacker

## When to Seek Medical Care

### Call 9-1-1 (Emergency)
- Loss of consciousness
- Difficulty breathing
- Chest pain
- Severe bleeding
- Head injury with confusion
- High fever with altered mental status

### Go to Urgent Care
- Minor injuries (sprains, strains)
- Minor burns or cuts
- Mild allergic reactions
- Moderate fever
- Ear infections

### Use First Aid Kit
- Minor cuts and scrapes
- Blisters
- Headaches
- Insect bites/stings
- Sunburn
