# Student Arrival Day Procedures - UC Berkeley Summer Springboard 2026

## Timeline Overview

### Preparations (9:00am-12:00pm)

#### Assigned Tasks: Swag and Check-in Setup
**Mentor Assignments Required**

Activities:
- Prep swag bags
- Put name tags in lanyards and organize in alphabetical order
- Hang name tags on dorm doors
- Set up tents and tables as workstations in courtyard
- Put up signs (entering courtyard by streets)
- Use blue painter's tape to make arrows on sidewalks from streets to courtyard

### Organize Workstation Tables (12:30pm-1:30pm)

#### Assigned Tasks: Workstation Organization
**Mentor Assignments Required**

Set up the following stations:

1. **Check-in Station**
   - Computers for check-in
   - Ensure student information is correct/completed

2. **Swag Station**
   - SSB Lanyard with name tags
   - Bags
   - T-shirts

3. **Card and Key Station**
   - PEX cards
   - Meal cards (Crossroads)
   - Keys
   - Fobs

4. **Academic Uniform Station**
   - **High School Scrubs:** Emergency Medicine, Pre-med, Surgery & Pediatrics
   - **High School Lab Coats:** Biotechnology & Nursing
   - **Middle School Scrubs:** Medicine

### Setup Music/Speaker, Games, Tables for Dinner (12:30pm-1:30pm)

#### Assigned Tasks: Entertainment and Dining Setup
**Mentor Assignments Required**

- Set up large speaker for music and announcements in courtyard
- Set up variety of games in courtyard area and main lounge
- Arrange tables for dinner service

### Airport Duty (11:00am-5:00pm)

#### Assigned Tasks: Airport Pickup
**Mentor Assignments Required**

**Requirements:**
- Wear SSB orange staff t-shirt and lanyard
- Get SSB signs and first aid kits from office
- Take picture ID
- Get list of names of students picking up and their phone numbers from office
- Message students in advance, including a photo

**Pickup Procedures:**
- Wait for students in baggage claim area UNLESS they are unaccompanied minors
- If unaccompanied minor: report to airlines desk and follow their instructions
- Have students call parents to let them know you have them

## Student Arrival (2:00pm-5:30pm)

### Residential Students: 2:00pm-5:30pm
### Commuter Students: 4:00pm-5:30pm

### Workstation Operations

#### Station 1: Check-in and Information Verification
**Mentor Assignments Required**
- Check in students
- Ensure student information is correct/completed

#### Station 2: Swag Distribution
**Mentor Assignments Required**
- Hand out SSB Lanyard with name tags
- Distribute bags
- Distribute t-shirts

#### Station 3: REACH Station
**Mentor Assignments Required**

#### Station 4: Cards and Keys Distribution
**Mentor Assignments Required**
- Assign PEX cards

- Distribute keys
- Distribute fobs

#### Station 5: Academic Uniform Distribution
**Mentor Assignments Required**

Distribute by course:
- **High School Scrubs:** Emergency Medicine, Pre-med, Surgery & Pediatrics
- **High School Lab Coats:** Biotechnology & Nursing
- **Middle School Scrubs:** Medicine

### Student Engagement

#### Engagement Tasks (Ongoing During Arrival)
**Mentor Assignments Required**

Activities:
- Engage students in games
- Interact with quiet, isolated students
- Introduce students to one another

#### Room Escort Tasks
**Mentor Assignments Required**

Responsibilities:
- Show students/parents to dorm room
- Demonstrate fobs to enter building and operate elevator
- Point out main lounge and SSB office
- Escort to room; point out floor bathroom

## Post-Arrival Activities

### Icebreakers in Mentor Groups (5:30pm-6:00pm)

**All Mentors with Their Assigned Groups**

- Mentors gather students into their mentor groups
- Mentors lead icebreaker/get to know you exercises

### Dinner - Pizza & Salad in Mentor Groups (6:00pm-7:00pm)

**All Mentors with Their Assigned Groups**

- Mentors escort and sit with their mentor groups at dinner
- Mentors continue to build rapport and get to know students during dinner

### All Student "Light" Orientation (7:00pm-7:45pm)

**All Mentors with Their Assigned Groups**

- Mentors sit with students in mentor groups
- Mentors model good large group behavior and help manage students

### Commuter Students Leave (7:45pm-8:00pm)

**Mentor Assignments Required**

- Mentors check out commuter students using REACH
- Mentors remind commuter students to be at Freeborn Hall at 8:30am on Monday to check in and attend All Student Orientation

### Residence Unit 1 and Freeborn Hall Tour (8:00pm-8:30pm)

**All Mentors with Their Residential Student Groups**

#### Tour Includes:
- **Laundry room**
  - Point out how machines work
  - Help with downloading the app if needed
- **SSB office**
- **Trash and recycle room**
- **Kitchen**
- **Courtyard**
- **U1 Front Desk**
- **Gym**

#### Mentor Reminders to Students:
- What time and where to meet for breakfast walk to Crossroads dining hall
- Complete Room Condition Reports (RCR) during unstructured time
- Complete Roommate Contracts (RC) during unstructured time
- RCR and RC will be checked during bed checks

### Unstructured Time (8:30pm-9:30pm)

**All Mentors on Duty**

**Student Rules:**
- Students have unstructured time
- Cannot leave Residence Unit 2
- Must be in groups of three

**Mentor Responsibilities:**
- Make themselves available and visible
- Help students open RCR and RC
- Ensure students are safe and following rules

### Back to Room and Bed Checks (9:30pm-10:30pm)

**All Mentors with Their Assigned Groups**

**9:30pm-10:00pm:**
- Students return to dorm rooms to get ready for bed (shower, etc.)

**10:30pm - Bed Checks:**
Mentors conduct bed checks and:
- Check in with students
- Verify RCR & RC completion
- Do a quick "Rose and Thorn" or another check-in exercise

### Team Huddle (10:30pm-11:00pm)

**Campus Director, Academic Manager, SPA, HQ Staff, and All Mentors**

Agenda:
- CD, AM, SPA, HQ Staff, and mentors debrief
- Report any shout outs or concerns
- Go over Monday's schedule/assignments
